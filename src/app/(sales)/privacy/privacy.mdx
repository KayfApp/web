## Collection and Use of Information:
To enhance your experience and deliver our services, Kayf.app collects and processes the following user information:

- **Homepage Analytics:**\
    Kayf.app uses **Google Analytics** to gain insights into our customer base. This includes collecting anonymized information such as device type, browser type, and general location data (e.g., city-level geography). This data helps us understand usage patterns and improve our services. For more details, please visit [Google’s official page on data usage](https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites).

- **User-Provided Information:**
  - Email address
  - Full name
  - Provided username
  - Pages or content created by the user

- **Plugin Ecosystem:**\
Plugins within Kayf.app may collect additional personal data. Each plugin provides specific details regarding the information it collects, how it’s used, and its privacy practices. Users can access this information directly within the plugin’s documentation or settings panel.

- **Self-Hosting:**\
If you choose to host Kayf.app yourself, no user data is stored on Kayf’s servers.
